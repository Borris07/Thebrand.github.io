Bare feet

The Women First organization has designed Bare Feet, a program aimed at identifying and restoring hope amongst learners who don't have school shoes, shoes that are worn-out, or in critical condition. Women First is committed to providing all the happiness and support these kids deserve through a special program of identifying and donating these shoes to them. The program is responsible for collecting data and photographs of assets. All imagery taken shall not be publicly advertised. All engineers and volunteers should stick to Women First protocols on media publishing. Shoes accompanied by socks of matching school should be distributed.
